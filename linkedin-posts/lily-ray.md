Post 1 – Google and AI Content Quality

Summary:  
Lily Ray discusses how Google continues to prioritize high-quality, trustworthy content despite the rise of AI-generated articles. She emphasizes that AI content alone is not the issue—low-quality, unedited AI content is.

Key Points:
    Google evaluates content based on E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness)
    AI content must be reviewed and enhanced by humans
    Mass-produced AI pages without value are being devalued

Insights:
    AI is not a shortcut; it’s a multiplier
    Human editing is a ranking factor in practice
    Quality signals matter more than content volume
